- Filmmakers scoring their projects
- Podcast producers needing intro/outro music
- Content creators on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram
- Game developers seeking soundtracks
- Advertising agencies finding commercial music
- Anyone needing high-quality production music

### How is Blendtune different from other music platforms?

Blendtune focuses on production music for creative projects with:
- Advanced filtering by key, tempo, mood, and instruments
- Real-time waveform visualization
- Detailed metadata for each track
- Licensing information for commercial use
- Professional-grade audio quality
- Tools designed for creative workflows

## Account & Access

### Do I need an account to use Blendtune?

Yes, you need a free account to access the music catalog and use the player features.

### How do I create an account?

1. Click "Sign Up" in the navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Verify your email address
4. Start browsing the catalog

### I didn't receive my verification email

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes for delivery
- Click "Resend verification email" in account settings
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Contact support if still not received

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to `/auth/reset-password`
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and enter a new password

### Can I use Blendtune on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your account from any device with a web browser and internet connection.

## Player & Playback

### Why isn't the audio playing?

**Troubleshooting steps:**
- Check browser allows audio playback
- Ensure device volume is not muted
- Verify internet connection is stable
- Try refreshing the page
- Try a different browser
- Check if other websites play audio

### What is the waveform visualization?

The waveform shows a visual representation of the audio's amplitude over time. You can click on it to seek to specific positions in the track.

### What is the equalizer showing?

The equalizer displays real-time frequency analysis, showing the intensity of different frequency ranges (bass, mids, treble) during playback.

### Can I control playback with my keyboard?

Yes, keyboard shortcuts include:
- Spacebar: Play/Pause
- Left/Right arrows: Seek backward/forward
- Up/Down arrows: Volume control
- M: Mute/unmute

### Why is playback choppy or buffering?

**Common causes:**
- Slow internet connection
- Too many open browser tabs
- Outdated browser
- Network congestion

**Solutions:**
- Close unnecessary tabs
- Check internet speed
- Update your browser
- Try again during off-peak hours

## Filtering & Search

### How do I find tracks in a specific key?

1. Go to the Sounds page
2. Open the Key filter
3. Select your desired key (C, D, E, F, G, A, B)
4. Choose Major or Minor scale

### What is BPM?

BPM (Beats Per Minute) measures the tempo/speed of a track. Higher BPM = faster, lower BPM = slower.

### What does "half-time" mean?

Half-time makes a track feel half as fast. For example, a 140 BPM track feels like 70 BPM. Useful for finding tracks that work at different perceived tempos.

### Can I combine multiple filters?

Yes! You can use multiple filters simultaneously for precise results. For example, filter by Genre, Key, Tempo, and Mood all at once.

### Why am I getting no results?

- You may have conflicting filters applied
- Try removing some filters to broaden your search
- Check filter values are set correctly
- Try alternative keywords

### Which browsers are supported?

**Recommended:**
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

### Does Blendtune work on mobile?

Yes, Blendtune is fully responsive and works on both iOS and Android mobile devices.

### What is a Progressive Web App (PWA)?

Blendtune can be installed as a PWA, which allows:
- Faster loading times
- Offline access to some features
- App-like experience on mobile
- Installation to home screen

### Why is the site slow?

**Possible causes:**
- Slow internet connection
- Server high traffic
- Browser cache issues
- Outdated browser

**Solutions:**
- Clear browser cache
- Update browser
- Check internet speed
- Try incognito/private mode

### I'm getting error messages

Common errors and solutions:

**"Session expired"**
- Sign in again
- Your session timed out for security

**"Unable to load tracks"**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try again in a few minutes

**"Playback failed"**
- Track may be unavailable
- Try a different track
- Clear browser cache
