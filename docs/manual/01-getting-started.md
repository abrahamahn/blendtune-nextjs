# Getting Started with Blendtune

This guide will help you create an account and start exploring Blendtune's music catalog.

## Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**
   - Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up" in the navigation
   - Or go directly to `/auth/signup`

2. **Enter Your Information**
   - Email address (must be valid for verification)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters recommended)
   - First and last name
   - Accept terms of service and privacy policy

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - Your account will be activated

4. **Sign In**
   - Return to `/auth/signin`
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"

## Navigating the Platform

### Main Pages

- **Home (`/`)**: Welcome page with platform overview
- **Sounds (`/sounds`)**: Main music catalog with filtering and playback
- **Account**: Access your profile and settings

### User Interface Elements

**Header**
- Logo: Click to return home
- Navigation menu
- User account menu (when signed in)
- Sign in/Sign up buttons (when signed out)

**Footer**
- Links to privacy policy and terms of service
- Contact information
- Social media links

## Exploring the Catalog

1. **Go to the Sounds Page**
   - Click "Sounds" in the navigation
   - Or navigate to `/sounds`

2. **Browse Tracks**
   - Scroll through the catalog grid
   - Each card shows track information:
     - Track title
     - Artist/creator name
     - Genre and mood tags
     - Duration
     - Key and tempo

3. **Play a Track**
   - Click on any track card
   - The audio player will open and begin playback
   - Waveform visualization appears

## Understanding the Interface

### Track Cards
Each track card displays:
- **Cover Art**: Visual representation
- **Title**: Track name
- **Artist**: Creator name
- **Metadata**: Genre, tempo (BPM), key, mood
- **Duration**: Total track length

### Player Controls
- **Play/Pause**: Start or stop playback
- **Volume**: Adjust audio level
- **Seek**: Jump to specific time in track
- **Waveform**: Visual representation with interactive seeking

## Tips for New Users

1. **Use Filters**: The filtering system helps you find exactly what you need
2. **Explore Genres**: Try different genres to discover new sounds
3. **Check Metadata**: Key, tempo, and mood help match music to your project
4. **Save Favorites**: (Feature coming soon) Bookmark tracks you like
5. **Email Verification**: Make sure to verify your email for full access

### Recommended Specifications
- Broadband internet (3 Mbps or faster)
- Modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
- Speakers or headphones for audio playback

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In?
- Check your email and password are correct
- Make sure you've verified your email
- Try resetting your password

### Audio Not Playing?
- Check your browser allows audio playback
- Ensure speakers/headphones are connected
- Try refreshing the page

### Slow Performance?
- Check your internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
