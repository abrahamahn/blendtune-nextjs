# Account Management

Learn how to manage your Blendtune account, update your profile, handle security settings, and manage your subscription.

## Account Overview

Your Blendtune account provides:
- Personalized music recommendations
- Playlist creation and management (coming soon)
- Download history tracking (coming soon)
- Subscription management
- Security and privacy controls

## Accessing Account Settings

1. Sign in to your account
2. Click your profile icon/name in the header
3. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown
4. Navigate through different settings sections

## Profile Information

### Personal Details

**Editable Information**:
- First name
- Last name
- Email address
- Profile picture (coming soon)
- Bio/description (coming soon)

**How to Update**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Profile
2. Click "Edit" next to the field
3. Enter new information
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Email Verification

**Why Verify?**
- Required for full platform access
- Enables password reset functionality
- Ensures you receive important notifications

**How to Verify**:
1. Check inbox for verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to confirmation page

**Resend Verification Email**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Email
2. Click "Resend Verification Email"
3. Check your inbox (and spam folder)

## Security Settings

### Password Management

**Changing Your Password**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click "Update Password"

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (recommended)
- At least one number (recommended)
- At least one special character (recommended)
- Cannot be a common password

### Password Reset

**If You Forgot Your Password**:
1. Go to `/auth/reset-password`
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter new password
7. Confirm new password
8. Click "Reset Password"

**Troubleshooting**:
- Check spam/junk folder for reset email
- Verify you entered correct email address
- Link expires after 24 hours - request new one if needed
- Contact support if issues persist

### Active Sessions

**Viewing Sessions**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. View "Active Sessions" section
3. See all devices where you're logged in

**Session Information**:
- Device type and browser
- IP address
- Location (approximate)
- Last active timestamp

**Ending Sessions**:
1. Find session you want to end
2. Click "Sign Out" next to that session
3. Or click "Sign Out All Other Devices" for security

**When to End Sessions**:
- Used a public/shared computer
- Lost or sold a device
- Notice suspicious activity
- Regular security maintenance

## Subscription & Billing

### Subscription Plans

**Available Plans** (example structure):
- **Free**: Limited access to catalog
- **Pro**: Full catalog access, higher quality audio
- **Enterprise**: Team features, API access

**Viewing Current Plan**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Subscription
2. View plan details, features, and billing cycle

**Upgrading/Downgrading**:
1. Click "Change Plan"
2. Select new plan
3. Review changes
4. Confirm upgrade/downgrade
5. Update billing information if needed

### Billing Information

**Payment Methods**:
- Credit/debit cards
- PayPal (if available)
- Other payment providers

**Updating Payment Method**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Billing
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter new card details
4. Click "Save"

**Billing History**:
1. View past invoices
2. Download receipts
3. Check payment status

### Cancellation

**To Cancel Subscription**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Provide feedback (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Access continues until end of billing period

## Privacy Settings

### Data Collection

**What Data is Collected**:
- Account information (email, name)
- Usage statistics (tracks played, search queries)
- Session data (IP address, user agent)
- Cookie preferences

**Managing Privacy**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy
2. Review data collection policies
3. Toggle optional data collection on/off
4. Download your data
5. Request data deletion

### Cookie Preferences

**Cookie Types**:
- **Essential**: Required for site functionality
- **Analytics**: Help improve user experience
- **Marketing**: Personalized recommendations (optional)

**Managing Cookies**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy
2. Click "Cookie Preferences"
3. Toggle cookie categories
4. Save preferences

## Notifications

### Email Notifications

**Notification Types**:
- Account security alerts
- New music releases
- Subscription renewals
- Marketing emails (optional)
- Newsletter (optional)

**Managing Notifications**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Notifications
2. Toggle each notification type on/off
3. Set frequency (immediate, daily digest, weekly)
4. Save preferences

**Unsubscribe**:
- Click "Unsubscribe" link at bottom of any email
- Or manage in Account Settings

## Account Data

### Downloading Your Data

**What You Can Download**:
- Profile information
- Playlist data (when available)
- Listening history (when available)
- Account activity logs

**How to Download**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy
2. Click "Download My Data"
3. Confirm request
4. Receive email when ready (may take 24-48 hours)
5. Download archive file

### Deleting Your Account

**Before You Delete**:
- Download any data you want to keep
- Cancel active subscriptions
- Note that deletion is permanent

**Deletion Process**:
1. Go to Account Settings > Account
2. Scroll to "Delete Account" section
3. Read warnings carefully
4. Enter password to confirm
5. Click "Delete My Account"
6. Confirm via email link

**What Happens After Deletion**:
- Account is permanently deleted
- Personal data is removed (some data retained for legal compliance)
- Access to content is revoked
- Subscriptions are cancelled
- Action cannot be undone

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Access Account Settings
- Make sure you're signed in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Check for service outages

### Email Not Updating
- Verify new email is valid
- Check verification email in spam folder
- Contact support if not received

### Payment Issues
- Verify card details are correct
- Check card has not expired
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try different payment method
- Contact your bank if declined

### Verification Email Not Received
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes for delivery
- Request new verification email
- Verify email address is correct
- Contact support

## Best Practices

### Security
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (when available)
- Regularly review active sessions
- Don't share account credentials
- Sign out on shared devices

### Privacy
- Review privacy settings regularly
- Only enable necessary data collection
- Download your data periodically
- Read privacy policy updates

### Billing
- Keep payment method up to date
- Review bills for unexpected charges
- Cancel before renewal if not continuing
- Save receipts for tax purposes
